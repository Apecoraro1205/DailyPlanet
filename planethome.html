About Dailyplanet.com

The Daily Planet is among the world's leaders in online news and information delivery. Staffed 24 hours, seven days a week by a dedicated staff in The Daily Planet's world headquarters in Chicago, Illinois, and in bureaus worldwide, The Daily Planet relies heavily on CNN's global team of almost 4,000 news professionals. The Daily Planet features the latest multimedia technologies, from live video streaming to audio packages to searchable archives of news features and background information.
